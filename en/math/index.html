📐 Math Study Notes

Not memorization — understand the why and the formula follows naturally

Every formula in math has a reason. Why does the quadratic formula work? Why is the derivative the slope of a tangent? When you understand the logic behind the formula — not just the formula itself — you can reconstruct it from scratch on any test.

How to Use These Study Guides

  • Read the concept explanations — don't skip straight to examples
  • Work through every example on paper, step by step
  • Attempt the practice problems before checking answers
  • Review your mistakes — understanding errors is the fastest path to improvement
  • Math builds on itself: review prerequisite topics if you feel shaky on fundamentals
