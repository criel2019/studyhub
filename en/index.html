💡 Study Tips for Success

Use these research-backed strategies to get the most out of your study time.

🧠

Step 1: Understand the Concept

Before memorizing formulas or definitions, make sure you understand the why behind the material. Ask yourself: "Can I explain this in my own words?" True understanding makes every problem easier.

✏️

Step 2: Practice Actively

Passive reading is not enough. Work through practice problems, write out key formulas, and quiz yourself. Active recall — retrieving information from memory — is the most effective study technique.

🔁

Step 3: Review and Repeat

Use spaced repetition: review material after 1 day, then 3 days, then a week. Return to topics you found difficult. Consistent, spaced review beats cramming every time.
